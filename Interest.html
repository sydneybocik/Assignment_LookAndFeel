CHAMPA BAY

Tampa Bay Buccaneers

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers (colloquially known as the Bucs) are a professional American football team based in Tampa, Florida. The Buccaneers compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the league's National Football Conference (NFC) South division, and play its home games at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa. The Bucs have won 2 Super Bowl Championships: 2002 and 2020. The club is owned by the Glazer family.

Championships

  • Super Bowl championships (2): 2002 (XXXVII), 2020 (LV)
  • Conference Championships (2): 2002, 2020
  • Division Championships (7): 1979, 1981, 1999, 2002, 2005, 2007, 2021

Tampa Bay Lightning

The Tampa Bay Lightning (colloquially known as the Bolts) are a professional ice hockey team based in Tampa, Florida. They compete in the National Hockey League (NHL) as a member of the Atlantic Division in the Eastern Conference, and play their home games at Amalie Arena in Downtown Tampa. The Lightning have won three Stanley Cup championships: 2004, 2020, and 2021. They also reached the Stanley Cup Finals in 2015. The team is owned by Jeffrey Vinik, and the general manager is Julien BriseBois. Jon Cooper has served as head coach since 2013, and is the longest-tenured active head coach in the NHL.

Championships

  • Stanley Cups (3): 2003 - 2004, 2019 - 2020, 2020 - 2021
  • Conference Championships (3): 2003 - 2004, 2014 - 2015, 2019 - 2020
  • Presidents' Trophy (1): 2018 - 2019
  • Division Championships (4): 2002 - 2003, 2003 - 2004, 2017 - 2018, 2018 - 2019

Tampa Bay Rays

The Tampa Bay Rays are an American professional baseball team based in St. Petersburg, Florida. The Rays compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the American League (AL) East division. Since its inception, the team's home venue has been Tropicana Field.

Championships

  • World Series Titles (0)
  • American League Pennants: 2008, 2020
  • American League East Division Titles: 2008, 2010, 2020, 2021

Tampa Bay Rowdies

The Tampa Bay Rowdies are an American professional soccer team based in St. Petersburg, Florida. The club was founded in 2008 and first took the pitch in 2010. Since 2017, the Rowdies have been members of the USL Championship in the second tier of the American soccer pyramid. They formerly played in USSF Division 2 (in 2010) and the North American Soccer League (NASL) (from 2011 to 2016), which were also second-tier leagues. The Rowdies play their home games at Al Lang Stadium on St. Petersburg's downtown waterfront.

Championships

  • League (2) - 2012, 2020 (Co-Champion due to Covid cancelling Championship game)
  • USL Season Champion (1): 2021
  • USL Division Champion (2): 2020, 2021
  • SL Conference Champion (2): 2020, 2021
  • Ponce de Leon Cup (2): 2010, 2012
  • Coastal Cup (7): 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2016, 2020, 2021
